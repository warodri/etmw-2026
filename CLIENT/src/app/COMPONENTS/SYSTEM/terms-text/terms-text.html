Where ETMW produces the audiobook (AI narration, human narration, or hybrid), ETMW owns the specific mastered audio production file.

4. Distribution Restrictions

The ETMW-produced audio file may only be distributed via ETMW. Authors may not request or redistribute the mastered production file.

Authors remain free to create and distribute independently produced audiobook versions elsewhere.

5. Revenue Share

70% of net subscription revenue is allocated to the creator pool. Distribution is calculated proportionally based on verified listening time.

ETMW retains 30% for platform operations.

Payments are made periodically subject to minimum payout thresholds. Authors are responsible for their own tax obligations.
